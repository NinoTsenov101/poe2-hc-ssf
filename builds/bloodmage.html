Blood Mage

Flameblast Ignite

Your life is the price. Pay it willingly.

HC Viable High Damage Advanced

Overview

The Blood Mage turns fire and blood into a single devastating weapon. This build channels Flameblast to release catastrophic ignites, then layers curses — Despair weakening enemies to chaos damage, Temporal Chains slowing everything around you — before detonating all accumulated hexes with Hexblast for a single enormous chaos burst.

The HC challenge: Flameblast requires you to stand still and channel. In HC, standing still gets you killed. Mastering this build means knowing exactly when it is safe to channel — and when to run. The payoff is enormous damage that trivialises endgame content when played correctly.

SSF note: The core loop (Flameblast + Despair + Hexblast) works on rares. No mandatory uniques, but good fire damage and life rolls on gear will dramatically increase your margin for error.

Pros & Cons

✔ Pros

  • Massive Flameblast ignite damage
  • Hexblast melts bosses instantly
  • Temporal Chains slows all enemies
  • Despair amplifies all damage significantly
  • No mandatory uniques

✘ Cons

  • Channeling is dangerous in HC
  • Fire-resistant enemies reduce damage
  • Reflect maps are instantly lethal
  • Mana hungry — careful flask management needed
  • Advanced build — not beginner friendly

Key Skills

Flameblast

Your primary damage skill. Channel to build stacks, then release for a massive fire explosion that applies a devastating ignite. The bigger the channel, the bigger the ignite.

Incinerate

Sustained fire beam for continuous ignite stacking. Use this to keep ignites ticking on groups of enemies while you reposition for the next Flameblast.

Despair

Your main curse. Increases chaos damage taken by the target — which amplifies Hexblast enormously. Apply before every boss fight, no exceptions.

Blasphemy + Temporal Chains

Blasphemy converts Temporal Chains into a permanent aura that permanently slows every nearby enemy. This is your safety net — slower enemies give you more time to channel safely.

Hexblast

Detonates all curses on a target for massive chaos damage. Your boss killer. Stack Despair and Temporal Chains on the boss, then unload Hexblast for a devastating burst.

⚠ HC SSF Specific Tips

  • Never channel Flameblast without a clear escape route — one bad position is a RIP
  • Temporal Chains slows enemies but doesn't stop one-shots — always maintain distance
  • Apply Despair before every boss encounter — the damage amplification is not optional
  • Reflect maps will kill you instantly — skip them, always
  • Keep a life flask with instant recovery on your left mouse button, not buried in your belt
  • Fire resistance at 75% before maps. Chaos resistance matters too for deep endgame.
  • When in doubt, use Hexblast first on dangerous rares — eliminate the threat before it eliminates you
