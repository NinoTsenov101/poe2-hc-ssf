Infernalist

Flame Wall Ignite

Safe. Strong. SSF-friendly.

HC Viable SSF Friendly League Starter Beginner Friendly

Overview

The Infernalist Flame Wall Ignite is one of the safest league starters for HC SSF. You ignite enemies through Flame Wall, then let the damage over time do the work while you stay at a safe distance. No chase uniques required — rares carry you to endgame.

The HC appeal: you're rarely in melee range. Most deaths in HC come from being surrounded — this build keeps you moving and at distance.

Pros & Cons

✔ Pros

  • Excellent clear speed
  • Safe playstyle — ranged DoT
  • No mandatory uniques
  • Strong league starter
  • Good single target

✘ Cons

  • Rippy against reflect maps
  • Ignite immune bosses are rough
  • Requires flask management
  • Scales heavily with investment

Key Skills

Flame Wall

Your primary skill. Creates a wall of fire that ignites anything passing through it.

Firestorm

Boss damage. Fire projectiles through the wall to ignite and melt single targets.

Flame Dash

Your movement skill. Escape dangerous situations instantly. In HC — use it often.

⚠ HC SSF Specific Tips

  • Always cap your fire resistance before maps (75%)
  • Keep a life flask with bleed removal at all times
  • Log out at the first sign of a dangerous volatile mob
  • Do not do reflect maps — ever
  • Level your Flame Dash quality to reduce cooldown
